Vission & Mission Statement

The Vision of Army Welfare Education Society is 'Quality Education at Affordable Cost'. To ensure a holistic development of the Child Army Welfare Education Society has launched a series of initiatives.They are:

  • Our Vision
  • Computer Aided Learning
  • Managing the challenges of Adolescence
  • Herbal Gardens in every school for awareness of medicinal plants
  • Science Parks
  • Faculty Enrichment Programmes to include English Language Teaching
  • Games, sports, hobby centers and activity clubs
  • Life saving skills
  • Adventure Camps

The Vision of Army Welfare Education Society is 'Quality Education at Affordable Cost'. To ensure a holistic development of the Child Army Welfare Education Society has launched a series of initiatives.They are:

  • Our Mission
  • Augmenting Educational facilities to meet the needs of children of Army Personnel including Widows and Ex- Servicemen (Army).
  • Preparing the students for All India Secondary School and All India Senior School Certificate examination of the Central Board of Secondary Education
  • Promoting Computer Aided Learning. Applications of Computers as well as expectation fromteacher continue to grow. CAL in school is not only to develop computer literacy amongst the children but is also an effective tool in the hands of the competent teachers.
  • Conducting English Bridge Course for the first generation learners.
  • Managing the Challenges of Adolescence.
  • Developing Herbal Garden in the school for the awareness of medicinal plants.
  • Developing Science Parks.
  • Conducting Faculty Enrichment Programmes
