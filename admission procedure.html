Admission Procedure

Students/Children will be admitted based on Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school, without an admission test:

• Children who are coming on Pass/Promoted TC from another APS/KV within the period 01 April to 31 July
• Children of Army Personnel coming on posting to the station from schools other than Army Public School/KV
• An evaluation may be taken in respect of these children to facilitate further academic coaching and allocation of class.
SUBJECTS AND MARKS OF ENTRANCE TEST ARE AS FOLLOWS

Primary Classes (II to V)
Middle Classes (VI to VIII)
Secondary Classes (IX to X)
Senior Sec. Classes (XI to XII)
English, Hindi, Maths 25
English, Hindi, Maths and Science 25
English, Hindi, Maths and Science 25
English and Elective Subjects 25

ADMISSSION PRIORITY

• Army Public School Transfer Cases.
• Transfer Cases from KV/Other private recognised schools.
• Transfer Cases from Local Schools
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION

• Transfer Certificate in original should be countersigned by appropriate authority (Distt Education Officer/ Regional Officer CBSE) except for APSs/KVs.
• Progress Report of the last school attended along with character certificate.
• Defence Personnel Serving Certificate (if applicable).
• Dependency certificate in respect of the children of serving personnel.
• Two passport size photographs.
• Residential Proof.
• Adhar Card
• Discharge Book copy
• Bank Passbook copy
ADMISSION TO CLASS XI

• Fresh admission will be taken in class XI after class X.
• The minimum percentage for different streams for admission in class XI is as per AWES guidelines.
• Meeting the eligibility criteria on account of minimum percentages, do not guarantee admission.
• Admission will depend upon relative availability of seats.
• No special weightage shall be given for any stream to any student.
