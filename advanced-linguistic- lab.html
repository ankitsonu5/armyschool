Advanced Linguistic Lab

Army Public School, Varanasi has advanced linguistic lab which is really outstanding to perform better upliftment of English enrichment for primary up to higher secondary students. We are focusing to improve every part of language skills at the grass root. We think language skill is very important part of English study so that we have installed an advance language learning suit which is Sanako-1200 that offers an extended range of essential activities that focus on both listening comprehension and voice practice. Study 1200 provides the students and teachers with all the features for and benefits of a modern language learning environment.

It is a pure software solution, study 1200 does not require any additional hardware and is therefore easy to install and maintain. Study 1200 multimedia teaching environment includes all the features and functions that are essential for effective and enjoyable teaching and learning.
